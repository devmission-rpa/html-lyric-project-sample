Billie Jean

by Michael Jackson

"Billie Jean" is a famous 1982 song by Michael Jackson from his album Thriller. The lyrics tell the story of a woman claiming the narrator is the father of her child, which he denies. Jackson stated the song was inspired by groupies who made similar claims about him and his brothers during their time with the Jackson 5.

Key Information

  • Release Date: January 2, 1983
  • Writer: Michael Jackson
  • Producers: Michael Jackson and Quincy Jones
  • Genre: Post-disco, rhythm and blues, funk, and dance-pop
